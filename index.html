Our Craft

Origin

72% Cacao
from Oaxaca

Sourced from a single cooperative in the Oaxacan highlands. Beans harvested at the precise moment of peak fermentation — never before, never after.

Process

Hand-tempered
in small batches

Our chocolatiers temper each batch by hand on marble slabs, controlling crystallisation with patience that no machine can replicate. Never mass-produced.

Sustainability

Carbon-neutral
process

Certified carbon-neutral from farm to wrapper. We offset every gram of our footprint and partner only with cooperatives who share our commitment to the earth.
